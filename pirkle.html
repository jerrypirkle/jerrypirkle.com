DESCRIPTION

Cloud security, operations, and technology enthusiast, writer, open source advocate, and a certified linux and infosec professional that loves learning and leading others to knowledge.

OPTIONS

Option Text

--L linkedin
my LinkedIn account. This is the best way to reach out to me for professional opportunities. I love what I'm doing, but I consider all interesting or compelling offers.

--s serverless
This site was created to test out AWS's serverless architecture.

--b bashfu
This is saved commands / bash fu.
